Refund Policy

Last updated: 2025

No Refunds

All sales on NoveJet are final. Due to the nature of our product, we do not offer refunds, returns, or exchanges once an order has been placed.

Why We Do Not Offer Refunds

  • RC jets and aircraft are flight-based products that can be damaged through handling or usage.
  • To ensure safety, hygiene, and performance reliability, items cannot be resold once opened.
  • Our fulfillment process begins immediately after the order is placed.

Order Cancellations

Orders cannot be cancelled once they have been submitted, as processing begins instantly.

Damaged or Incorrect Items

If your product arrives damaged or you receive the wrong item, please contact us within 48 hours at support@novejet.com with photo/video proof. We will resolve the issue with a replacement shipment when applicable.

Chargebacks

Initiating false chargebacks is considered fraud. We work with Razorpay and card networks to dispute unjustified claims.
